The Best three movies according to Tanish

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

Shawshank Redemption

"The Shawshank Redemption" is a movie about a banker wrongly imprisoned, who befriends a fellow inmate and escapes while teaching the value of hope and friendship.

Eternal sunshine of The spotless mind

In "Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind" , Joel and Clementine "the characters" choose to erase their memories of a failed romance.
As the past fades, they rediscover their love story within the labyrinth of their minds.
The film beautifully captures the idea that even when memories are gone, the essence of love endures, like a hidden treasure waiting to be unearthed.

Tu Hai Mera Sunday

"Tu Hai Mera Sunday" is a 2016 Indian film about a group of friends who find solace in playing football on Sundays, exploring themes of friendship and urban life's challenges.
